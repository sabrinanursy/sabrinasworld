About Me

I am Sabrina, an energetic Junior college student-athlete for over 10 years who has consistently balanced the demands of competitive basketball with maintaining an exemplary academic record.

Get to know my journey!
SIMPLE WORDS, BIG IMPACT

My coach once told me, "Please don't stop doing this, you have big potential." That moment sparked a fire within me, and since then, basketball has lived in my soul, always and forever.

My basketball journey began when I was in the 4th grade, a time when most kids were just beginning to discover their hobbies. For me, it was the moment I found my true passion. Representing my city at such a young age, I had the incredible opportunity to compete and earn my first gold medal.

IT’S NOT EASY

The journey to achieve all of my basketball accomplishments and become the person I am today has been anything but easy. I’ve poured my heart and soul into basketball, dedicating countless hours, blood, sweat, and even tears to reach this point. The journey has been marked by more than just the thrill of victories; it has also been defined by the sting of countless losses, moments of deep frustration, and the overwhelming pain of rejection--like the time I didn't make it to the DKI Jakarta (capital city of Indonesia) national team.

Go to My Big Achievement!

I WANT IT, I GOT IT

After years of hard work and building my basketball reputation, I was finally called by the national coach to participate in the East Jakarta national selection team. Initially, I was shocked and felt unworthy of being among such talented players. However, with encouragement and support from my senior, Kak Nindya, I persevered despite previous rejection. Despite coming from a lesser-known area, I pushed through and eventually earned a spot as one of the top players representing East Jakarta on the national stage.

The Power of Personal Branding

Being selected in the most competitive study abroad program for college students in Indonesia from 16,000+ applicants. Achieving this milestone has been far from easy. It’s not just about submitting four well-crafted essays, improving my English proficiency, or excelling in the interview process. It also involves creating a personal brand that stands out among 16,000 other applicants.

To be recognized as a potential awardee, I knew I needed to offer something unique. That’s why I’ve emphasized my identity as a student-athlete, a role that has required me to develop strong time management skills, balancing academics, practice, and competition. This experience reflects my ability to stay organized, disciplined, and focused, qualities that make me a strong candidate for this prestigious opportunity. By showcasing the dedication and resilience needed to succeed as a student-athlete, I am confident that I demonstrate the potential and capability to excel as one of their selected awardees.
